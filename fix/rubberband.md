<p>First go to GPS JoyStick settings and click on "<strong>Select Background Thread</strong>" and change it to Java. Start the app and check if rubberband is gone. If it still happens follow this:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>Open Magisk and go to Modules and delete the Smalipatcher module then reboot.</li>
<li>Open Magisk Manager again and go to Download section.</li>
<li>Search for "Riru" or get it <a href="https://github.com/RikkaApps/Riru/releases/download/v19.5/magisk-riru-core-v19.5.zip" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a></li>
<li>Install Riru - core and click back (don't click reboot after installation click back)</li>
<li>Now again Goto Download section in Magisk</li>
<li>Search "Edxposed" (You'll get 2 options)</li>
<li>Install Edxposed (YAHFA). If nothing shows up get it <a href="https://github.com/ElderDrivers/EdXposed/releases/download/0.4.5.1/magisk-EdXposed-YAHFA-v0.4.5.1_beta.4463.-release.zip" target="_blank" rel="noopener">here</a>.</li>
<li>Click Reboot when installed.</li>
<li>Install and Open Edxposed installer app. Download <a href="https://github.com/solohsu/XposedInstaller/releases/download/Ed_v2.2.4/EdXposedInstaller_v2.2.4-release.apk" target="_blank">here</a></li>
<li>Go to download section and Search "Fake Mock Location"</li>
